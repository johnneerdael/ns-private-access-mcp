title: Search Tools
date: 2026-05-20
---

Search tools find private apps and publishers quickly before deeper inspection or changes.

| Tool | Use it for |
|------|------------|
| `searchPrivateApps` | Search apps by name, host, tag, protocol, and association. |
| `searchPublishers` | Search publishers by name, region, status, or metadata. |

## Safe prompts

```text
Search for all private apps related to payroll, include hostnames and tags, and identify which publishers serve them. Do not change anything.
```

```text
Find disconnected publishers in Europe and summarize which private apps might be affected.
```
